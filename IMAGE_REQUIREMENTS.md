# TurboLoop Banner Image Requirements

This document outlines the exact specifications for all marketing banners required by the TurboLoop automation system and marketing hub.

## 1. Zoom Call Banners (24 Total)

These banners are used for daily Telegram reminders for the global Zoom calls. They must be visually distinct to prevent ad fatigue.

**Dimensions:** 1200x630px (16:9 Landscape)
**Format:** PNG (High Quality)
**Logo:** Official TurboLoop logo (Swirl + Wordmark) in the top-left corner.

### Required Variants
For each of the following 4 tiers, we need **3 visually distinct variants** (different layouts, backgrounds, and themes) in both **English** and **Hindi** (Total: 4 tiers × 3 variants × 2 languages = 24 banners).

| Tier | Badge Text | Key Information to Include |
|---|---|---|
| **T-60** | 1 HOUR | "Daily Community Call", "Global · English · 5 PM UTC", "turboloop.tech" |
| **T-30** | 30 MIN | "Daily Community Call", "Global · English · 5 PM UTC", "turboloop.tech" |
| **T-15** | 15 MIN | "Daily Community Call", "Global · English · 5 PM UTC", "turboloop.tech" |
| **LIVE** | LIVE NOW | "Daily Community Call", "Global · English · 5 PM UTC", "turboloop.tech" |

*Note: For Hindi variants, replace "English · 5 PM UTC" with the appropriate Hindi call details (e.g., "Hindi/Urdu · 3 PM UTC").*

### Design Themes (Mix and Match)
1. **Neon Cyberpunk:** Dark navy, glowing cyan/purple energy, tech focus.
2. **Liquid Glass:** Premium finance, smooth chrome/glass ribbons, elegant.
3. **Cosmic Deep Space:** Galaxy vortex, infinite scale, epic mood.
4. **Clean Minimalist:** Maximum negative space, massive typography, corporate.
5. **Data Analytics:** Rising charts, glowing nodes, financial growth.

## 3. General Design Rules for All Banners

1. **No "APY":** Never use the term "APY". Use "Total ROI" or "Daily Returns".
2. **Accurate Stats:** Only use approved stats: "54% Total ROI in 60 Days", "0.9% Daily Returns", "No Impermanent Loss", "Start with 1 USDT".
3. **Legibility:** Text must be highly legible on mobile devices. Use dark overlays behind text if the background is busy.
4. **Consistency:** The logo must always be present, but the layout and background should vary significantly between variants to prevent ad fatigue.
